# Clipin AI

**Create AI short videos from a single idea.**

[Clipin AI](https://clipin.ai) is an AI video generator for creators, marketers, and product teams. Turn prompts, images, scripts, and product ideas into ready-to-share short videos with AI-generated scripts, scenes, voiceovers, captions, music, and clips.

## What Clipin Helps You Make

- AI short videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts
- Text-to-video and image-to-video clips
- Product demos, social ads, educational explainers, and creator content
- Storyboards, reference images, thumbnails, and campaign visuals

## Platform

Clipin combines video generation, image generation, and image editing in one browser-based creative workspace. Start with an idea, review the generated structure, refine scenes, and create short-form video assets without a traditional editing timeline.
